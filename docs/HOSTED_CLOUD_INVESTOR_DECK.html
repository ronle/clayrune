Business model

BYOK. We never
touch tokens.

The user pays their AI provider directly. We never mark up or meter model usage — so the most volatile, dangerous line in AI economics is simply not on our books. We charge a transparent, bucketed hosting fee for storage, compute and network. No metering. No bill-shock.
